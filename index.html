// faq

Is this a real airdrop?

Points are tracked off-chain now and will be made claimable at TGE. No allocation is guaranteed; the team reserves the right to filter sybil activity.

When is the snapshot?

No fixed date. Snapshot will be announced ~7 days in advance on the Coyoti channels. Stack early — the multiplier favors early referrers.

Do I need to connect a wallet to refer?

No. Just paste an address to generate a link. To claim later you'll need to sign with that address.

Which chain?

Coyoti is live on Solana. Token: 4u7K…pump on pump.fun.

How are sybils filtered?

Standard heuristics — funding-graph clustering, behavior similarity, on-chain age. Don't try it. We'd rather give the allocation to real users.
