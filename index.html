// how points work

  • 10 pts when a referee connects a wallet through your link.
  • 1 pt per $1 of TVL they bring to StackFi (vaults, leverage, farms).
  • 5% of every point your direct referrals earn — forever.
  • 1% of points from their referrals (2nd degree).
  • 1.5×–3× multiplier applied based on rank (Bronze → Diamond).
  • Self-referrals, sybils, washed wallets get zeroed at snapshot.

// faq

Is this a real airdrop?

Points are tracked off-chain now and will be made claimable at TGE. No allocation is guaranteed; the team reserves the right to filter sybil activity.

When is the snapshot?

No fixed date. Snapshot will be announced ~7 days in advance on the Coyoti / StackFi channels. Stack early — the multiplier favors early referrers.

Do I need to connect a wallet to refer?

No. Just paste an address to generate a link. To claim later you'll need to sign with that address.

Which chain?

StackFi is live on PulseChain and Avalanche. Referrals count across both.

How are sybils filtered?

Standard heuristics — funding-graph clustering, behavior similarity, on-chain age. Don't try it. We'd rather give the allocation to real users.
